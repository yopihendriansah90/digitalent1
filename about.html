About Us

Technology company and strategic partner for digital transformation.

PT. Systech Talenta Digital, known as DigiTalent, focuses on two core services: IT Training and IT Outsourcing. We believe digital progress depends on skilled people who can adapt and perform in real environments.

DigiTalent is part of SGI Asia Group, an IT group established in 2013. We originated from the training division of PT. Systech Global Informasi and later became an independent company.

With strong industry experience and networks, DigiTalent addresses two key needs: developing competent professionals and providing industry-ready talent.

Our Commitment

Bridge the gap between industry demands and talent availability through structured training programs and flexible, trusted outsourcing services.

Vision

Leading strategic partner for superior digital talent.

To be the leading strategic partner in developing and providing superior, innovative, and globally competitive digital talent to support international-standard digital transformation.

Mission

  • Provide high-quality IT training and certification programs.
  • Establish a professional and reliable outsourcing ecosystem.
  • Forge strategic collaborations with industry, education, and government.
  • Drive continuous innovation in learning, recruitment, and project execution.
  • Uphold integrity, professionalism, and client satisfaction.
